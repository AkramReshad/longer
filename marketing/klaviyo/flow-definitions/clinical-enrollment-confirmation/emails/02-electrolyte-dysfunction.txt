WHAT IS ELECTROLYTE DYSFUNCTION?

Electrolyte Dysfunction, commonly abbreviated ED, occurs when physical demand exceeds what water alone can support.

COMMON PRESENTING CONDITIONS

01 — Heat, sweat, and prolonged exertion
02 — Mile 20 and the hours beyond it
03 — Pregame, postgame, and next morning
04 — Cramps, fatigue, and reduced duration

The Longer development program is evaluating a legitimate electrolyte stick pack for these moments. The intended result is simple: credible hydration performance delivered with pharmaceutical seriousness.
